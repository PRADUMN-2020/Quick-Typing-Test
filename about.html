About

Type it off is a quick 1-minute typing speed test. Here you can test your typing skills. Just start typing to get your typing results.

Words

Type it off uses about 2000 English words to generate the typing tests.

Result Screen

After the 1 minute is completed, the user will is directed to a result screen that gives the result of their typing test. The result screen contains the following parameters.

Accuracy - This is the percentage of correctly typed characters.

Raw Speed - It is the total number of words typed in 1 minute. A word is any five characters. In raw speed, the incorrectly typed characters are also included.

Net WPM - It is similar to raw speed, but only the correctly typed characters are considered while calculating net wpm.

CPM - It is simply the total number of characters typed in 1 minute, including the wrong ones.

Key bindigs

You can press Ctrl + R anytime to restart the typing test. Or you can use the try again button after the test is ended to try again.
